A bit more context

I build data systems that turn complexity into clarity, and increasingly, into intelligence. I'm most energized when the data is messy, the problem is open-ended, and the answer isn't obvious yet.

I'm pursuing an MS in Information Systems at Northeastern, specializing in Data Analytics, BI, and Data Engineering. I work across the full data stack: SQL and Python pipelines, dashboards, and predictive models built on datasets from 10,000 to 50 million rows.

Before grad school, I spent two years as a Business Intelligence Analyst at Vaishnav Publications and Digitalization: SQL, Python, Power BI, and Tableau across 5+ client accounts, automating reporting and building dashboards that shaped budget decisions.

I'm drawn to the intersection of data engineering and AI, building the pipelines that make machine learning work in production instead of just in notebooks. I'm especially interested in consulting, finance, and healthcare, where data quality shapes real outcomes.

When I'm not in front of my laptop, you'll find me at the pool table or trying out a new recipe in the kitchen.
