A bit more context

I'm a data analyst and aspiring data engineer pursuing my MS in Information Systems at Northeastern, with a computer engineering background from the University of Mumbai. My work sits at the intersection of messy real-world data and the clean, reliable systems businesses need to make decisions.

For two years I worked as a Business Intelligence Analyst at Vaishnav Publications and Digitalization, moving from dashboard maintenance to owning end-to-end BI delivery — data models, automated reporting pipelines, and the numbers that shaped campaign decisions.

Outside coursework, I build projects that mirror production problems: multi-million-row pipelines, medallion architectures, models tuned past 0.95 ROC-AUC. I'm drawn to the engineering side of analytics — schema design, query optimization, reliable tooling at scale. I'm looking for Data Analyst, Data Engineer, or BI Engineering co-ops and full-time roles.
